1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our Services, including which features you use.

GymLens Privacy Details

This section provides specific privacy details for GymLens, our AI-powered fitness app.

Data Collection Specific to GymLens

  • Equipment Photos: Images you take or upload of workout equipment for analysis. These are processed to generate workout recommendations.
  • Fitness Preferences: Your fitness goals, limitations, and equipment preferences.
  • Workout History: For subscribers, we store your workout history to track progress.
  • Device Information: Information about your device to optimize app performance.

How We Use GymLens Data

  • Generate personalized workout routines

Health Disclaimer: GymLens is not a medical device and does not store or process protected health information ("PHI") as defined by HIPAA. Always consult with healthcare professionals before starting any new exercise program.

How We Use Eggsplore Data

Prompt Doctor Privacy Details

Data Collection Specific to Prompt Doctor

  • Prompt Entries: The AI prompts you submit for analysis and optimization.
  • Prompt History: Your previously analyzed prompts are stored to help you track improvements.
  • Prompt Preferences: Your custom settings for prompt generation and analysis.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device to optimize app performance.

How We Use Prompt Doctor Data

  • Analyze your AI prompts to provide quality and effectiveness feedback
  • Generate suggestions to improve your prompts
  • Save your prompt history for future reference
  • Improve our prompt analysis algorithms
  • Track the effectiveness of suggestions through user feedback

Prompt Data Security

We understand that your prompts may contain sensitive information or ideas. Important Note: Prompt Doctor does not have access to your conversations with other AI systems such as ChatGPT or Claude. We only analyze the prompts you explicitly submit to our app.

Martina Privacy Details

This section provides specific privacy details for Martina, our professional pet care scheduling and route management app.

Data Collection Specific to Martina

  • Pet Information: Names, service types, and care requirements for pets you serve.
  • Client Data: Client names, addresses, contact information, and service history.
  • Visit Schedules: Appointment times, service types, duration, and completion status.
  • Location Data: Client addresses, your home base location, and route information for navigation and optimization.
  • Business Analytics: Visit completion metrics, route efficiency data, and service statistics.
  • Route History: Optimized routes, travel patterns, and navigation preferences.

How We Use Martina Data

  • Schedule and organize pet care visits
  • Generate optimized routes for efficient travel between clients
  • Store client and pet information for ongoing service relationships
  • Track visit completion and service history
  • Provide navigation assistance to client locations
  • Enable data export for business record-keeping
  • Improve route optimization algorithms

Professional Data Protection

We understand that as a professional pet care provider, you handle sensitive client information. All data is stored locally on your device with optional iCloud backup. Client information, pet details, and business data are never shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes. You maintain complete control over your business data.

Location Privacy

Location data is used exclusively for navigation and route optimization purposes. We do not track your location when the app is not in use. Your home base location and client addresses are stored securely and are only accessed when providing routing services.

Third-Party Services for Martina

  • Google Gemini API: Used for AI-powered route optimization and scheduling suggestions
  • Apple Maps: Provides navigation services and real-time traffic information
  • iCloud: Optional backup and synchronization of your data across devices
  • Apple StoreKit: Manages app purchases (when applicable)

Data Export and Portability

You can export your scheduling data, client information, and visit history at any time through the app's export functionality. This ensures you always have access to your business records and can transfer data to other systems as needed.

Business Continuity: Martina is designed to support your professional pet care business. Your data remains under your control, with local storage and optional cloud backup ensuring your business information is always accessible and secure.
